Euro 2020 - Terms of Use

Group Stage

  • Includes 36 matches.
  • Participants to place bets on match results (90 minutes).
  • Points to be calculated as follows:
    • 1 pts for ‘direction’ (1, X, or 2)
    • 3 pts for ‘BOOM’ (accurate match result)

      • *No more than 3 points can be given per game.

Knockout

  • Includes 15 matches.
  • Participants to place bets on match results (90 minutes).
  • When placing a bet on a draw, one must also place a bet on ‘direction’ (e.g: winner of extra time / penalties).
  • Points to be calculated as follows:
    • 1 pts for ‘direction’ (1, or 2).
    • 4 points for ‘BOOM’ (accurate match result after 90 minutes).

      • *No more than 4 points can be given per game.

Top Scorer/ Assists

  • Prior to the group stage, each participant will place a bet on the top 3 goalscorers + top 3 ‘Chefs’ for the entire tournament For each of those players entering into the final top 3 (regardless of where in the top 3), participants will gain 3 points.
  • Example: one’s bet - 3 top goalscorers: Kane, Griezmann, Fred. If only Fred and Kane enters the top 3 goalscorers, one will gain 6 points total, regardless if they are 1st, 2nd or 3rd.

Tie Breakers

  • 1. Total points.
  • 2. Total # of BOOMs.
  • 3. Total # of goals scored in one’s BOOMs.
  • 4. The success rate in the tournament.
  • 5. Group stage ranking.

Compliance

  • Bets will be placed separately before each stage (Groups, top-16, Quarter, Semi, Finals).
  • Bets will be placed until 12 hours before stage's first match.
  • In case that user didn't place its bets on time, results will be generated randomly.

Prize Distribution

  • 1. 65%
  • 2. 25%
  • 3. 10%

    • **Please Note - In case that we will also play the EU2020 PoloCup, 20% of the total prize goes to the CUP winner, and the rest splits as stated above.
